Implementation Details

  • Sequential Counting: Numbers count from 000 to their target value one by one
  • Right-to-Left Flip Order: Digits flip from right to left like a mechanical counter
  • Clean Design: No backgrounds or gradients - just amber-500 colored text
  • Smooth Animation: Vertical flip with subtle blur effect during transition
  • Timing: Optimized speed to showcase the flip effect while maintaining readability

Test Values: 100% (counts to 100), 500+ (counts to 500), 50+ (counts to 50), 75% (counts to 75)
